Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a restaurant recommendation?

Use the contact form above and select "Restaurant Recommendation" as the subject. Please include the restaurant name, location, and what makes it family-friendly!

Can restaurants partner with you?

Absolutely! We offer featured listings and partnerships for family-friendly restaurants. Select "Partnership Inquiry" from the subject dropdown and we'll get back to you with details.

How do I update my newsletter preferences?

Every newsletter includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. If you have other questions about your subscription, contact us using the form above.

Do you cover areas outside the Bay Area?

Currently, we focus exclusively on the San Francisco Bay Area. However, we're always considering expansion! Let us know if you'd like us to cover your area.
