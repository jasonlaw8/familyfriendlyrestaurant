Our Review

A cozy family diner with a dedicated kids play area and a menu that appeals to all ages. The patient staff goes above and beyond to make families feel welcome.

The Little Dipper Diner has quickly become a neighborhood favorite for families. What sets it apart is the thoughtful attention to detail - from the colorful play area visible from most tables to the complimentary coloring books and crayons brought to every table with kids.

The menu offers classic American comfort food with healthy options for kids (not just the typical chicken nuggets!). Parents love the outdoor patio with misters for those warm San Francisco days, and the noise-friendly atmosphere means you don't have to stress if your little one has a meltdown.
